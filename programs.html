Programs

BridgePoint operates across two tracks: Investment Research Competitions (sell-side) and Virtual Fund Management (buy-side).

1) Investment Research Competitions (Sell-side)

We support our competition representatives (“frontliners”) who represent both the community and UCSI University in high-level investment research competitions.

Support system

  • Training from lecturers and industry professionals
  • Structured peer discussions and collaboration
  • Strategic team grouping and preparation support
  • Curated reading materials and resources
  • Funding assistance (subject to rules)

Community sustainability

The community receives 10% of any cash prizes to support future training and initiatives. Competition outcomes also support branding and PR.

Why it matters

Frontliners build strong, real-world competencies: analytical thinking, financial modelling, communication, presentation, and critical reasoning — backed by measurable results.

Note: Members may participate in either Investment Research Competitions or Virtual Fund Management — not both.

2) Virtual Fund Management (Buy-side)

This initiative runs through dedicated fund teams led by a Fund Manager and supported by Analysts who contribute research and decision support.

How it works

  • Fund Manager sets strategy and portfolio direction
  • Analysts support through research, analysis, and idea generation
  • Reward & redemption system to recognise contributions

Why it matters

Members build credible “talking points” and a track record for buy-side interviews — decisions, wins/losses, and learning outcomes.

Note: Members may participate in either Investment Research Competitions or Virtual Fund Management — not both.
